Disabling and Highlighting Dates and Days

Disabling or highlighting dates and days can be done in a variety of ways. The pertinent options are:

blackDays
An array of days to disable, every week. 0 = Sunday, 1 = Monday, ... 6 = Saturday (e.g. [2])
blackDates
An array of ISO 8601 Dates to disable. (e.g. ["2011-01-01", "2011-12-25"])
highDates
An array of ISO 8601 Dates to highlight. (e.g. ["2011-01-01", "2011-12-25"])

Blacklisted Days

Example shows all Tuesdays disabled.


<label for="mydate">Some Date</label>

<input name="mydate" id="mydate" type="date" data-role="datebox"
   data-options='{"mode": "calbox", "calBlackDays": [2] }'>

Blacklisted Dates

Example shows all Christmas disabled.


<label for="mydate">Some Date</label>

<input name="mydate" id="mydate" type="date" data-role="datebox"
   data-options='{"mode": "calbox", "calBlackDates": ["2011-12-24", "2011-12-25"] }'>

Highlighted Dates

Example shows all Christmas highlighted.


<label for="mydate">Some Date</label>

<input name="mydate" id="mydate" type="date" data-role="datebox"
   data-options='{"mode": "calbox", "calHighDates": ["2011-12-24", "2011-12-25"] }'>
